Thengai Thogayal

Ingredients

  1. Urad dhal
  2. Milagai Vetthal
  3. Tamarind
  4. Coconut

Instructions

  1. Fry 5 milagai vetthal and 1 tbsp. urad dhal in oil
  2. Add 1/2 tsp. salt after dhal reddens
  3. Add gooseberry-sized tamarind and 1/4 coconut (1/4 moodi)
  4. Grind in mixie
  5. Add water for desired consistency
