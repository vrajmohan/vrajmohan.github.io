---
title: Medhu vadai
---

## Ingredients
- Whole Urad Dhal (1/2 cup - 150g)
- 1/2 tsp asafoetida
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 green chillies
- 1 inch ginger

## Instructions

1. Wash urad dhal multiples times (5+) and soak in water for 1h
1. If using mixie, soak for an additional hour in fridge
1. Drain water and grind adding 2 tbsp water (3 times)
1. Add salt, asafoetida, green chillies and ginger
1. Fry in oil 5-6m
