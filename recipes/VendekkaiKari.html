Vendekkai Kari (Okra)

Ingredients

  1. Okra
  2. Mustard
  3. Turmeric powder
  4. Sambar powder
  5. Asafoetida

Instructions

  1. Splutter mustard in oil.
  2. Add okra, turmeric powder, sambar powder and salt.
  3. Add a little asafoetida.
  4. Toss in pan. Don't stir with a spoon.
  5. Cover and simmer
