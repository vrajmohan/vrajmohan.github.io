Pulikacchal

Ingredients

  1. Tamarind
  2. Gingelly oil
  3. Channa dhal
  4. Milagai Vetthal
  5. Curry leaves
  6. Peanuts
  7. Turmeric powder
  8. Jaggery
  9. Asafoetida

Instructions

  1. Soak orange-sized tamarind in water. Strain and rinse 3 times to extract the juice.
  2. Splutter mustard in 4 tbsp. gingelly oil.
  3. Add 1 tbsp channa dhal, 10 milagai vetthal, curry leaves, peanuts, 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder and the tamarind juice.
  4. Add a little jaggery, salt to taste and a piece of asafoetida.
  5. Stir and cook till paste is formed.
