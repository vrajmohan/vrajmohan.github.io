---
title: Upma Kozhakattai
---

## Ingredients
- Idly rice (2 cups)
- Coconut (1/2 moodi)
- Urad dhal
- Mustard seeds
- Curry leaves
- salt
- Coconut oil

## Instructions

1. Soak idli rice 2 cups. Grind it with coconut 1/2 moodi.
1. Add salt.
1. Heat coconut oil in a pan and pour the batter into it. Keep kindifying till the batter is half cooked and leaves the side of the pan. Will round up like a dough. Let it cool a bit.
1. Now make kozhakattai after wetting your palms.
1. Steam it. when you cut the kozhakattai the center should also be cooked
1. Tadka coconut oil/mustard seeds/urad dal and karuveppilai.
