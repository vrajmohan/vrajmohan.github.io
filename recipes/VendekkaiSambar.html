Vendekkai Sambar

Ingredients

  1. Vendekkai
  2. Capsicum
  3. Tamarind
  4. Sambar powder
  5. Asafoetida
  6. Toor Dhal

Instructions

  1. Splutter mustard in oil.
  2. Add okra and capsicum pieces and fry.
  3. Make paste out of lime-size piece of tamarind and 1 1/2 cups of water.
  4. Add tamarind water, 1 1/2 tbsp. Sambar powder and a liitle asafoetida
  5. Let it boil for 15 minutes under medium heat
  6. Add toor dhal made as paste from 2 karandi dhal cooked in 1 1/2 cups of water.
  7. Add curry leaves
  8. If sambar is sour, add jaggery
  9. If sambar too watery, add kadalai maavu mixed in water
  10. Drumsticks can be added without frying
