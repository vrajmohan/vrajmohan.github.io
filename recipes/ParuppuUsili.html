Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

  1. Toor dhal
  2. Milagai Vetthal
  3. Mustard

Instructions

  1. Soak 3/4 cup toor dhal and 4 milagai vetthal in water for 1 hour.
  2. Coarse grind in mixie.
  3. Add 1tsp. salt.
  4. Make paste into patties and place on idli plate.
  5. Cook in pressure cooker without using weight.
  6. Cook on high till steam starts to appear.
  7. Simmer till patties start to look solid.
  8. Splutter mustard in oil.
  9. Crumble the patties over the mustard+oil and mix.
