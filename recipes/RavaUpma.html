Rava Upma

Ingredients

  1. Rava
  2. Mustard
  3. Channa dhal
  4. Urad dhal
  5. Green chillies
  6. Onions/peas/potatoes
  7. Asafoetida
  8. Coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Dry roast 1 cup rava.
  2. Splutter mustard in oil.
  3. Add 1 tsp. channa dhal and 1 tsp. urad dhal.
  4. After the dhal reddens, add cut green chillies.
  5. Add onions/peas/potatoes and vadhakkufy.
  6. Add 1 1/2 cups water and bring to boil.
  7. Add the rava while constatntly stirring.
  8. Later while stirring, add salt and asafoetida.
  9. Add a drop of cocnut oil, cover and let cool for 3 minutes.
