Kozhambu Maavu Upma

Ingredients

  1. Milagai Vetthal
  2. Gingelly oil
  3. Channa Dhal
  4. Mustard
  5. Curry Leaves
  6. Asafoetida
  7. Rice flour

Instructions

  1. Splutter mustard in 1 tbsp. cooking oil and 1 tbsp. gingelly oil.
  2. After splutter, add milagai vetthal and channa dhal.
  3. Add curry leaves and 1/2 tsp asafoetida. (Add chopped onions if desired)
  4. Add 3cups of water and salt to taste.
  5. Wait 3 minutes after boiling.
  6. Add 1 1/2 cups of rice flour while continuously stirring.
  7. Smash any lumps that are formed.
  8. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Cover and let cool.
