Pongal

Ingredients

  1. Rice
  2. Mung dhal (payattam paruppu)
  3. Jeera
  4. Asafoetida
  5. Cashewnuts
  6. Ghee
  7. Pepper (whole)

Instructions

  1. Take 1 cup rice and 1/3 cup mung dhal.
  2. Dry heat till you get the aroma of pongal.
  3. Add 1tsp. jeera and cook with water.
  4. Pressure cook without weight till steam appears. Put weight after steam appears.
  5. Add salt and mix.
  6. Fry cahewnuts in ghee. Add broken pepper.
  7. Mix everything.
