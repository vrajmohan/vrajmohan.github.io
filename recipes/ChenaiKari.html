Chenai Kari (Yam)

Ingredients

  1. Yam
  2. Turmeric powder
  3. Mustard
  4. Milagai Vetthal
  5. Coconut oil
  6. Asafoetida

Instructions

  1. Peel the skin from the yam and cut into small cubes
  2. Add water, salt and turmeric powder and cook till the yam is soft enough to be squeezed
  3. Splutter mustard and 2-3 milagai vetthal in oil
  4. Add yam, a little coconut oil and asafoetida
  5. Cover and cook
